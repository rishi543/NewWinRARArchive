Overview

Core Resource Management System – The source for Efficiency and Growth

Cospectral flagship product CRMS is an integrated applications suite of comprehensive enterprise resource planning software that combines business value, standards-based technology, and deep industry experience into a business solution with a low total cost of ownership. It helps the businesses successfully compete in today’s global markets by providing the tools and technology to:

  • Capture all of your business information in a single, scalable system
  • Automate and accelerate your end-to-end business processes
  • Improve decision making and customer satisfaction with real-time insights
  • Supercharge business performance and analytics with add-on analytics tool
  • Get up and running in just 4 to 8 weeks – and customize as per your business need

CRMS help you with:

Lean manufacturing—Adopt lean processes across your enterprise and value chain.
Quality management—Improve quality to increase productivity and reliability.
Financials—Get greater financial visibility and insight.
Manufacturing—Tame the complexity of your manufacturing supply chain.
Process manufacturing—optimize your process manufacturing to protect profit.
Wholesale and distribution—make your extended supply chain more responsive.
Service management—manage your entire customer lifecycle.

Features:

  1. Customizable to your needs:

    CRMS has inbuilt world class Business Practices for different industries. You can even get them customized according to your business need. The Customizations of CRMS help increase adaptability of the ERP amongst the Users making it economical for the company.

  2. Simple and Easy to Use:

    CRMS has very user-friendly GUI and requires little navigation skills. Its intuitive design ensures that your employees get used to it within weeks.

  3. Standard integrated modules:

    CRMS has a number of standard integrated modules which can be customized to your Business practices in India, or any other country of your business operation. You can start with a few essential modules and then add more functions as need arises. This reduces your dependence on multiple applications and brings consistency in information sharing across the organization.

  4. Comprehensive Features per Module:

    CRMS has comprehensive features in every module, which means most of your business requirements can be easily fulfilled by standard CRMS ERP.

  5. Multi-company and Multi-location:

    CRMS can help you consolidate your businesses across multiple locations and geographies, thus making it economical for your business.

  6. Integrated with Third-Party Applications:

    CRMS is easy to integrate with a large number of 3rd party applications. If required, your existing applications/hardware can be made to communicate with CRMS.
